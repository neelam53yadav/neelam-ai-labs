About Me

I am an AI builder and problem solver who turns complex business problems into simple, usable, and production-ready AI products.

My work sits at the intersection of Generative AI, machine learning, cloud platforms, software engineering, automation, and enterprise architecture. I enjoy building systems that are useful, reliable, secure, and easy for people to adopt.

Through Neelam AI Labs, I share practical AI products, software utilities, web applications, AI experiments, and learning resources for professionals who want to use AI in real work, not only in theory.

Mission

To make AI useful beyond buzzwords — by creating practical, trustworthy, and accessible products that help people work smarter, automate better, and solve meaningful problems with confidence.
